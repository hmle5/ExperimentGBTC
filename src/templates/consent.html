Privacy policy

This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in accordance with GDPR. Our primary goal is to ensure your personal information remains protected and used transparently.

Data collection

We collect minimal data necessary for your participation in our survey, using an anonymized unique identifier generated from your IP address and user agent information. This data is not stored but temporarily used to ensure one-time participation.

Data use and retention

Data collected is solely for the analysis within this study. Per GDPR regulations, the collected data will not be retained beyond its immediate purpose. Unique identifiers are masked and non-identifiable.
