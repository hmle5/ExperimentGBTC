Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in accordance with GDPR. Our primary goal is to ensure your personal information remains protected and used transparently.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for participation in our survey, using an anonymized unique identifier generated from your IP address and user agent information. This data is not stored but temporarily used to ensure one-time participation.

Data Use and Retention

Data collected is solely for survey purposes and analysis. Per GDPR regulations, data will not be retained beyond its immediate purpose. Unique identifiers are masked and non-identifiable.

Your Rights
  • You have the right to access your data and to request its deletion.
  • You can object to data processing practices that concern you.
  • For any concerns, contact us at vwl@tu-braunschweig.de.
Data Protection

Your data is protected using industry-standard methods. We do not share your personal data with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you of any significant changes.

For detailed information, please visit Official GDPR Guidelines.
